They are Innocent

Hello World! Welcome to the website for They are Innocent. It's a project to help spread stories about wrongful convictions. Seeds of Innocence is a part of the project. The point of Seeds of Innocence is to make these makeshift trees from cardboard with information about wrongful convictions for anyone to take off the branches. The birds symbolize the innocence of those wrongly convicted. A bird uses its wings for flight to move far and wide. I hope you can spread this information beyond this cardboard tree! Please tell others, and if you have a story about someone wrongly convicted please submit it below. It can be on the next tree for others to read. This tree is to spread the stories of those who have had their innocence taken from them despite being innocent. Thank you for your time!
